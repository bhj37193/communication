Charisma Trainer ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile app for practicing real-time text conversations with an AI character and getting feedback on your communication skills. This page explains what data we collect, why, and how to have it deleted.

We do not collect: your precise location (only your general timezone, to know your local day for streaks), contacts, photos, browsing history, advertising identifiers, or any content from messaging with other real users. There is no user-to-user messaging, feed, or public profile in this app — you interact only with an AI character.

Tracking

We do not track you across other companies' apps or websites for advertising purposes, and no third-party advertising SDK is present in the app.

Data retention

  • Chat transcripts and session results are automatically and permanently deleted 60 days after creation.
  • If you delete your account, every record associated with it (sessions, transcripts, results, progress, analytics events, usage, entitlements, and your profile) is permanently deleted right away, in one operation.

How to delete your data

Deleting your account from within the app (or by contacting us, see below) permanently deletes all of your data as described above. This action cannot be undone.

Children's privacy

Charisma Trainer is not directed at children and is not knowingly used by children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
