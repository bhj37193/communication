# POSITIONING (locked 2026-07-03)

The consumer chat communication app. Consolidates the positioning work (this session +
Fable's brief in FABLE-PROMPT-POSITIONING.md). No em-dashes.

## The line
**"Charisma is a skill. Train it."**

Hero idea (the paradox, the manifesto): **The most interesting person in the room is the
one who makes everyone else feel interesting.**

## Category
A **conversation trainer**. Explanation sentence: "Duolingo for talking to people." Do
NOT create a category, borrow Duolingo's already-educated frame. Familiarity beats
novelty for a solo, zero-CAC founder. (Category creation is a Series-A luxury.)

## The wedge (the moat, lead with it)
**Charisma is a measurable, trainable skill, not a personality you are born with.** The
deterministic engine scores concrete signals (question quality, follow-up depth, talk
balance, reciprocity, listening, callbacks) the same way every run, so a user watches a
real number move week over week. No competitor can make the "measurable" claim: they all
give vibes-based LLM feedback that changes run to run, and the charisma-content world
(Charisma on Command, etc.) gives tips with no measured practice or progress.

## Beachhead
The charisma / magnetism self-improvement identity: people who have felt like the
forgettable or invisible one and want to be magnetic and memorable. Non-stigmatized,
shareable, aspirational, and it rides a proven organic content lane (Charisma on Command
6M+, Vanessa Van Edwards, etc.). Dating is a SCENARIO PACK, not the identity, so we get
dating intent without PUA coding of the brand.

Honest tradeoff (accepted): we trade acute intent-density (dating's "my matches are dying"
is searchable and urgent) for reach and zero stigma. Therefore growth runs on ASPIRATION
and IDENTITY (charisma content, the paradox hook, shareable progress), not on solving an
acute searchable pain. Content quality carries growth, not search intent.

## Differentiation, in one line each
- vs reply-generators (Rizz, ChatGPT-as-ghostwriter): they rent you a line and abandon
  you on the real date; we build the skill and you keep it.
- vs ChatGPT generic: measured practice with progression, not a one-off improv partner.
- vs charisma content (YouTube/books): measured practice and a moving score, not tips.
- vs Character.AI: we optimize for you LEAVING the conversation better; they optimize for
  you staying. Sessions end, feedback lands, streak advances. (Also inoculates against the
  companion-app safety narrative.)
- vs Duolingo / Speak: they own languages; nobody owns conversation. We are the adjacent
  category they validated, not a competitor.

## Name
DROP "Cadence" (collides with SalesLoft Cadence + Salesforce cadences; smells B2B).
Qualities: short (1-2 syllables), back-and-forth/verbal or practice energy, a 22-year-old
can say it out loud unembarrassed, ownable domain + App Store search, NOT dating-coded
(no match/spark/charm), safe to expand. Directions to mine: the rally/volley family (the
back-and-forth of a good exchange, doubles as a racket-sport practice metaphor), or a
coined 2-syllable word (the Duolingo/Speak route). Verify trademark classes 9 and 42.

## Traps to avoid (ranked)
1. The "be the performer/star" misread of "most interesting." FIX: the paradox is the
   whole teaching, the most interesting person makes others feel interesting. The product
   trains curiosity/reciprocity, not performance.
2. Getting coded as a pickup tool. FIX (structural): never generate lines to copy, always
   score the user's own words, publish the rubric, include obviously-non-seduction
   scenarios. The rubric (reciprocity, question quality) is anti-PUA by construction.
3. Feeling like homework. FIX: Character.AI-grade dramatic, specific, slightly absurd
   characters. Fun to fail.
4. Companion drift (coming back for the character, not the practice). FIX: enforce session
   endings, rotate characters, let feedback break the fourth wall.
5. Going broad too early ("better communicator" is oatmeal). FIX: hold the charisma /
   most-interesting identity tightly for year one.

## Positioning statement
For anyone who has felt forgettable in a room and wants to be magnetic, **[Name]** is a
conversation trainer that makes charisma a measurable skill you build in five minutes a
day, unlike charisma tips and reply-generators that leave you no better than before,
because its deterministic engine scores the real signals (questions, follow-ups,
reciprocity, balance) so you watch yourself actually improve.
