# Skill: storytelling [STUB]

id: storytelling
status: STUB

## Principle
Tell one short, concrete, vivid story (hook, struggle, resolution) instead of a vague
summary.

## WHY-IT-CONNECTS
Specifics (a named place, a number, a sensory detail) are what make a share feel real
enough to connect to. (Expand.)

## Observable signals
monologue_brag (max 0: a story is short or it is a monologue), future specifics signal.

## Failure modes
The rambling story (60+ words), the humble-brag disguised as a story.
