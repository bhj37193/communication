# Skill: make-the-ask [STUB]

id: make-the-ask
status: STUB

## Principle
End with a clear, low-pressure, concrete next step instead of letting a good
conversation evaporate.

## WHY-IT-CONNECTS
A connection with no next step is a nice memory; the ask is what turns it into a
relationship. (Expand.)

## Observable signals
future ask-present signal; final_warmth (an ask only lands on earned warmth).

## Failure modes
The premature ask (warmth not earned), the vague ask ("we should hang out sometime").
