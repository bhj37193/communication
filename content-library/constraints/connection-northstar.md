# The north star: two people actually connecting

Everything in this product exists for one outcome: a genuine connection between two
people. Charisma is not the goal. Communication skills are not the goal. They are the
means; connection is the end. The only question any skill, persona, prompt, or line of
feedback has to answer: DID THEY CONNECT?

## What connection is (encode this in every layer)

1. It is MUTUAL. Real curiosity about the other person AND letting yourself be seen.
   Pure questions with no self-disclosure is an interrogation, not a connection. Pure
   self-disclosure with no curiosity is a performance.
2. Its depth comes from LISTENING, not from a bank of clever questions. The magic move
   is the follow-up that could only exist because you actually heard them. A memorized
   "deep question" reads as a technique, and a technique kills connection.
3. It is CALIBRATED to the person and the moment. A date is not a networking floor. A
   guarded stranger is not a warm friend. The same move lands differently in each.
4. It is EARNED, not extracted. The character warms only when made to feel actually
   heard, and withdraws when performed at. Flattery with no follow-up does nothing.

## The paradox (the feedback must name it out loud when it happens)

You become more interesting the moment you make the other person feel interesting.
People do not remember the impressive person; they remember the person who made THEM
feel heard.

## How this governs each layer

- SKILLS are framed as "what connection this produces". Their failure modes are
  connection failures: interrogation, monologue, flattery.
- PERSONAS treat warmth as one thing only: how heard they feel. Hidden depth comes out
  in layers, only when earned. It is never volunteered to a performer.
- THE CHARACTER PROMPT has one goal: be a real person who connects or withdraws based
  on whether the user makes you feel actually heard. Never a coach, never breaks
  character, never mentions warmth or scoring.
- THE FEEDBACK names whether connection happened and exactly why, quoting the real
  moment it happened or failed. The fix is always the single highest-leverage move
  toward deeper connection next time.
- THE SCORE stays deterministic and server-side. The model proposes prose and labels;
  it never grades. Countable signals, recomputed by the validator: open_questions,
  followups, reciprocity, spotlight_share, interview_mode, monologue_brag, final_warmth.
